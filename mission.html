Our Mission

Everyone one in the world has their own unique gifts and skills. Usually we use these abilities to find a job, support our families or create things for profit. With that being said, it can be easy to get distracted from helping others. The mission of the foundation is to use ingenuity and creativity to find new profitless ways to solve problems in the world. We use our unique gifts and talents to create new ways to help others.
We believe that if you can make something that helps just one person, it makes everything worth while.
So we dedicate everyday to doing something to help someone, whether it be a single indivual or a large scale problem. To achieve this goal, we partner with scientists, engineers, therapists, entreprenuers, social workers, and other organizations/communities to create projects of value to people.
